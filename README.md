## 📱 About

BazaarIQ is an Android application designed for small vendors in village markets (Santhe) to manage credit (Udari), payments, and financial tracking digitally.

Built during internship to solve real-world problems faced by rural vendors who manage credit manually using paper ledgers.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **OTP Authentication** — Secure phone number login
- 💰 **Quick Udari Entry** — Add credit in 2 simple steps
- ✅ **Payment Recording** — Track customer payments
- 👥 **Customer Ledger** — View all customers with dues
- 📊 **Daily Summary** — Total sales and pending dues
- 📱 **WhatsApp Reminder** — Send payment reminders directly
- 🗄️ **Offline Support** — Works without internet (Room DB)
